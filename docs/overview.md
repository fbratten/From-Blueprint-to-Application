# Book Overview

> **From Blueprint to Application: The Complete Guide to Enterprise Prompt Engineering**
>
> By Fredrik Brattén & Saša Popović

---

## What This Book Is About

This book transforms the often frustrating experience of AI trial-and-error into consistent, time-saving workflows. Through a structured 90-day journey, you'll progress from understanding basic prompts to building enterprise-scale AI systems.

## The Red Thread: Sarah's Journey

Throughout the book, you'll follow **Sarah**—a professional who discovers prompt engineering and transforms her organization's approach to AI. Her story weaves through all 12 chapters, making complex concepts relatable and immediately applicable.

Sarah's journey mirrors your own:
- **Discovery**: Learning that AI communication is a skill, not luck
- **Mastery**: Building templates and establishing best practices
- **Leadership**: Driving organizational AI transformation
- **Innovation**: Implementing autonomous AI systems

---

## Book Structure

### Part I: Foundations of Prompt Engineering (Chapters 1-2)
Build your foundation with core principles and frameworks. Learn what separates amateur prompting from professional practice.

### Part II: The Enterprise Prompt Library (Chapters 3-4)
Establish a centralized library for your organization. Implement security, compliance, and governance.

### Part III: Engineering in Practice (Chapter 5)
Apply prompt engineering to specific domains. Customize for your industry and use cases.

### Part IV: Advanced Techniques (Chapters 6-8)
Master advanced methods: chain-of-thought, few-shot learning, and product development.

### Part V: Implementation Roadmap (Chapters 9-10)
Your 90-day action plan. From individual productivity to team transformation.

### Part VI: Infrastructure Evolution (Chapters 11-12)
The cutting edge: Model Context Protocol (MCP) and autonomous AI agents.

---

## How to Read This Book

### For Beginners
Start with Chapters 1-2, then jump to Chapter 9 for your 90-day plan. Return to other chapters as needed.

### For Intermediate Users
Skim Parts I-II for review, focus on Parts III-IV for advanced techniques.

### For Enterprise Leaders
Read Chapter 4 (Security), Chapter 9 (Implementation), and Part VI (Infrastructure) first.
